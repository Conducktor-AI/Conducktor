## Frequently Asked Questions

### When will I get access?

We're reviewing applications on a rolling basis and granting access in small batches. Expect to hear from us within 3-5 business days of submitting your request.

### Is there a cost during beta?

Beta access is **completely free** for the first 3 months. After that, you'll have the option to:
- Continue with beta pricing (50% off for 12 months)
- Upgrade to a paid plan
- Stay on the free tier (limited usage)

### What happens after the beta?

You'll have priority access to convert to a paid plan with exclusive beta pricing locked in for 12 months.

### Can I invite team members?

Yes! Once you have beta access, you can invite unlimited team members to your workspace.

### What if I have questions?

Email us at [beta@conducktr.com](mailto:beta@conducktr.com) with any questions about the beta program.

### Can I test the API before joining?

We have a sandbox environment available for approved beta testers. Request access via the waitlist form.
